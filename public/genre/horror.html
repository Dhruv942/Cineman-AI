How to Find Horror That Matches Your Taste

Horror is deeply personal. Some viewers want jump scares and supernatural terror (The Conjuring, The Exorcist). Others prefer slow-burn psychological dread (The Witch, It Follows, Hereditary). Slasher fans gravitate toward Halloween and The Texas Chain Saw Massacre. Knowing your preferred sub-genre is essential to finding horror that actually scares you rather than bores you.
