How to Find Crime Movies That Match Your Taste

Crime cinema comes in many forms. Mob epics like The Godfather and Goodfellas focus on organized crime families and their internal dynamics. Heist films like Heat and Reservoir Dogs center on the planning and execution of robberies. Procedurals like Zodiac and Se7en follow investigations from the law enforcement side.
