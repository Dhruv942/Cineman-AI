How to Find Thrillers That Match Your Taste

Thrillers come in many forms. Psychological thrillers like Gone Girl and Nightcrawler rely on unreliable characters and shifting perspectives. Crime thrillers like Se7en and Zodiac are driven by investigation and procedure. Survival thrillers like A Quiet Place and Wind River pit characters against hostile environments.
