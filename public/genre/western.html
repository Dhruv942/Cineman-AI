How to Find Westerns That Match Your Taste

Westerns range from classic spaghetti westerns (Leone's trilogy) to revisionist entries (Unforgiven, The Power of the Dog) to modern neo-westerns (Hell or High Water, No Country for Old Men). Action-oriented fans gravitate toward Django Unchained and Tombstone. Viewers who prefer slow-burn character studies enjoy The Power of the Dog and The Searchers.
