Fantasy ranges from epic high fantasy like Lord of the Rings to dark fairy tales like Pan's Labyrinth to whimsical Ghibli films like Spirited Away. Knowing whether you prefer Western or Eastern fantasy traditions, dark or lighthearted tones, and action or character focus narrows your search considerably.
