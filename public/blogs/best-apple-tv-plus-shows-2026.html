Best Apple TV+ Shows 2026 (With IMDb & Rotten Tomatoes Ratings)

Updated: May 9, 2026 12 min read

TL;DR

Apple TV+ punches far above its size. The 15 highest-rated shows on the platform — led by Severance, Slow Horses, Ted Lasso, and For All Mankind — average an IMDb score higher than any other major streamer’s top tier. To see those ratings as you browse, install CineMan AI.

Apple TV+ has spent the last six years quietly building one of the highest-quality television catalogs in streaming. The library is small — perhaps a tenth the size of Netflix’s — but the average rating is unusually high because Apple has prioritized prestige originals over volume. The result: if you scroll the Apple TV+ home screen and pick something at random, you have a much higher chance of landing on a 7.5+ IMDb show than you would on Netflix.

The Top Tier

1. Severance

The show that announced Apple TV+ as a serious prestige destination. Severance imagines a world where employees can surgically separate their work and personal selves — and follows what happens when those split selves start to reach across the divide. The slow-burn paranoia, the Stiller-Erickson visual rigor, and the Mark Friedman/Erickson script make Severance one of the smartest sci-fi series of the decade. Season 3 is in production for 2026.

2. Slow Horses

Slow Horses is the show people who love Slow Horses cannot stop telling everyone to watch. Gary Oldman is Jackson Lamb, the disgraced head of Slough House — the MI5 dumping ground for agents too embarrassing to fire. Six-episode seasons, deeply funny, deeply British, with espionage stakes that build rather than escalate. Adapted from Mick Herron’s novel series; Apple has confirmed enough seasons to cover the full book run.

3. Ted Lasso

The kindness comedy. Ted Lasso took a 2013 NBC Sports promo character and built him into a flagship fish-out-of-water sitcom about an American football coach managing an English Premier League team. Season three was divisive among critics (the IMDb score for that season is lower than the first two), but the cumulative effect across all three seasons remains one of the best comedies of the decade. A spinoff is reportedly in development.

4. For All Mankind

Apple TV+’s most underrated drama. The premise — what if the Soviets had won the moon race? — is the springboard for an alternate-history saga that tracks how that one divergence reshapes the next sixty years. Four seasons in, the show has covered the moon, Mars, and the alternate present. The spinoff Star City is launching later in 2026 (we have a full Star City preview here).

The Best Limited Series

Apple TV+ punches especially hard in the limited-series format. If you want something excellent in a single weekend, these all qualify:

  • Pachinko — technically multi-season, but Season 1 stands alone as a perfect 8-episode arc
  • Black Bird — 6 episodes, true-crime, devastating
  • Defending Jacob — 8-episode legal thriller miniseries
  • Lessons in Chemistry — 8-episode adaptation of Bonnie Garmus’s novel
  • Bad Sisters Season 1 — works as a contained 10-episode revenge story

None of these requires a multi-season commitment. You can finish the entire arc in a weekend.

Newer Hits Worth Mentioning

The Studio

Seth Rogen’s Hollywood satire was the runaway 2025 success of the platform. Eight episodes, each shot in a single elaborate take, lampooning everyone from method actors to studio executives to film directors. Season 2 is confirmed.

Silo

Post-apocalyptic adaptation of Hugh Howey’s book series. Methodical pacing, Rebecca Ferguson lead, plot mysteries that unfold across full seasons rather than within single episodes. If you liked the first half of Severance, you will like Silo.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the highest-rated show on Apple TV+?

Severance and Slow Horses share the top by composite score, both with 8.7+ IMDb and 97% Rotten Tomatoes. Ted Lasso has a slightly higher IMDb (8.8) but a lower Tomatometer.

Is Severance Season 3 on Apple TV+?

Season 3 is in production and confirmed for the 2026 slate. Seasons one and two are streaming now.

How much is Apple TV+ in 2026?

$9.99/month in the US, with discounts available via the Apple One bundle. Pricing varies by region.

Is Apple TV+ worth it in 2026?

For prestige-drama viewers, yes. The catalog is small but the average rating is unusually high. Pair it with HBO Max or Netflix for breadth.
