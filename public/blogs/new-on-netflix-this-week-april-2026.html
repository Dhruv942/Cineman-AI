Pick of the Week by Category

Best Prestige Drama: BEEF Season 2

Nothing else is close. See above.

Best Documentary: 180, A Gorilla Story

A David Attenborough-narrated 90-minute documentary following a Silverback gorilla troop over 18 months in Rwanda. Cinematography is staggering. Early Rotten Tomatoes reviews are among the best of any 2026 Netflix doc so far.

Best Light Watch: Running Point Season 2

Mindy Kaling's sports comedy returns. Not reinventing anything, but Kate Hudson is charming, the supporting cast is deep, and it is the kind of show you can actually enjoy while folding laundry. 84 percent on Rotten Tomatoes.

Skip: Thrash, Roommates

Thrash is a Category 5 hurricane / shark hybrid that leans more silly than fun. Roommates is a lower-tier comedy with limited laughs. Netflix will put both on your homepage. Do not let them.

Next Week's Headline: Stranger Things Tales From '85

The biggest animated release of the month drops April 23. A theatrical preview of Episodes 1 and 2 screens April 18 in select U.S. cities. The full eight-episode season hits Netflix on April 23. Read our Tales From '85 preview for full details.
