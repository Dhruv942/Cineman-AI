Bad Boys: Ride or Die — IMDb & Rotten Tomatoes Ratings + Where to Stream

Updated: May 29, 2026 9 min read

TL;DR

Bad Boys: Ride or Die (2024) holds a 7.0 IMDb and 65% Rotten Tomatoes (Tomatometer), with a remarkable 96% audience score. It is the fourth film in the franchise and Will Smith's first post-2022-Oscars commercial hit, grossing $404 million globally. Streams on Netflix in the US. Bad Boys: Ride or Die arrived in June 2024 with a heavier-than-usual job. The franchise's third film, Bad Boys for Life (2020), had successfully relaunched the series after a 17-year gap. But Ride or Die was the first Will Smith starring vehicle to hit theaters after the 2022 Oscars incident, and it was being released into a summer box office that had punished several would-be blockbusters in a row. It worked. The film opened to $56 million and ended its run at $404 million worldwide, the highest-grossing entry in the entire franchise.

Bad Boys: Ride or Die 2024 7.0 65% $404M

Ride or Die holds the highest IMDb rating of any film in the series — a remarkable achievement for a fourth installment. The Rotten Tomatoes critics score dipped slightly from the surprise-hit For Life, but the audience score is up. The pattern is unusual: franchises typically lose IMDb points as they extend.

What Critics Said

The 65% critical consensus split along familiar lines. The praise consistently mentioned the chemistry between Will Smith and Martin Lawrence (now four films and 29 years deep), Adil El Arbi and Bilall Fallah's stylized action direction (carried over from For Life), and the surprisingly emotional Mike Lowrey arc involving his estranged son Armando.

The criticism focused on a tonal whiplash: the film opens as a comedy, pivots into a corruption-thriller midway through, then escalates into a near-horror-style climactic sequence in a swamp compound. Some critics found this stretching of the formula refreshing. Others found it incoherent.

What Audiences Said

The 96% audience score is one of the highest of any 2024 wide release. Audience reactions consistently mentioned:

  • The Will/Martin chemistry still works. Three decades of friendship comes through on screen in a way that no chemistry workshop could manufacture.
  • The action sequences are inventive. El Arbi and Fallah's signature long-take, drone-camera, weapon-POV shots are the most visually distinctive in mainstream action filmmaking right now.
  • The emotional beats land. Mike's relationship with Armando and Marcus's reckoning with mortality both pay off in ways franchise sequels rarely attempt.
  • It is genuinely funny. Often missing in fourth-installment action movies.

Where to Stream Bad Boys: Ride or Die in 2026

United States: Bad Boys: Ride or Die streams on Netflix. Sony's pay-1 window licensed the film to Netflix in early 2025 following its initial Paramount+ run.

International:

  • UK: Sky Cinema / NOW
  • Canada: Crave
  • Australia: Stan
  • Germany / France / Spain: Netflix

Rent or buy: Available on Prime Video, Apple TV, Google Play, and YouTube for around $3.99 rental / $14.99 purchase.

Should You Watch Bad Boys: Ride or Die?

If you've enjoyed any previous Bad Boys film: yes. The 7.0 IMDb and the 96% audience score are not coincidence — this is the franchise's most-loved entry. The pacing is tighter than Bad Boys II, the action is more inventive than the original, and the emotional throughline gives it weight that pure-action sequels rarely earn.

If you've never seen a Bad Boys film: start with Bad Boys for Life (2020). It's the easiest entry point, requires no franchise context, and sets up the Mike/Armando relationship that pays off in Ride or Die.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the IMDb rating for Bad Boys: Ride or Die?

7.0 out of 10 from over 100,000 votes. Rotten Tomatoes: 65% critics, 96% audience.

Is Bad Boys: Ride or Die better than Bad Boys for Life?

Higher IMDb (7.0 vs 6.6), slightly lower RT critics (65% vs 75%). Most audiences consider it a solid continuation rather than a step up.

Where can I watch Bad Boys: Ride or Die?

Netflix in the US. Sky Cinema / NOW in the UK, Crave in Canada, Stan in Australia. Also available for rental or purchase on Prime Video, Apple TV, and YouTube.

Is there a Bad Boys 5?

A fifth film is in early development at Sony following Ride or Die's $404 million global box office. Smith and Lawrence both expected to return. No release date yet; production targeting late 2026 or early 2027.
