Should You Watch Princess Mononoke?

With an IMDb score of 8.3, Princess Mononoke is widely considered one of the best films in its genre. Critics and audiences agree this is essential viewing. The 93% Rotten Tomatoes score signals near-universal critical acclaim.

Ashitaka, a prince of the disappearing Emishi people, is cursed by a demonized boar god and must journey to the west to find a cure. Along the way, he encounters San, a young human woman fighting to protect the forest, and Lady Eboshi, who is trying to destroy it. Ashitaka must find a way to bring balance to this conflict.
