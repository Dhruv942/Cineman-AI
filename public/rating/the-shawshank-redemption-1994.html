Should You Watch The Shawshank Redemption?

With an IMDb score of 9.3, The Shawshank Redemption is widely considered one of the best films in its genre. Critics and audiences agree this is essential viewing. A 89% on Rotten Tomatoes indicates strong critical support.

Imprisoned in the 1940s for the double murder of his wife and her lover, upstanding banker Andy Dufresne begins a new life at the Shawshank prison, where he puts his accounting skills to work for an amoral warden. During his long stretch in prison, Dufresne comes to be admired by the other inmates -- including an older prisoner named Red -- for his integrity and unquenchable sense of hope.
